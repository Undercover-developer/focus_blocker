Focus Blocker

Focus Blocker helps you stay productive by blocking distracting or harmful websites using simple rules and optional schedules — all locally, with privacy first.

Install from Chrome Web Store Report an Issue

What Focus Blocker Does

  • Blocks websites you choose using domains, patterns, or categories
  • Supports optional schedules to block sites during specific times
  • Works instantly without slowing down your browser
  • No accounts, no tracking, no external servers

Why Install Focus Blocker

  • Stay focused during work, study, or deep-focus sessions
  • Reduce distractions from social media, news, or gambling sites
  • Keep all data on your device - privacy-first by design
  • Free, lightweight, and transparent

Privacy

Focus Blocker does not collect or transmit personal data. All settings are stored locally in your browser.
