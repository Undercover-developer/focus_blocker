Focus Blocker

Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2025-12-23

Summary

Focus Blocker is a website-blocking browser extension designed to help users stay focused by blocking distracting or harmful sites using user-defined rules and schedules. Focus Blocker does not collect, sell, or transmit personal data.

Data Collection

Focus Blocker does not collect or transmit any of the following:

  • Personally identifiable information (name, email, address, identifiers)
  • Browsing history, page titles, or contents of websites
  • Authentication credentials
  • Location data
  • Financial or payment information

How Your Settings Are Stored

Focus Blocker stores your configuration (blocked sites, custom rules, schedules, and preferences) in Chrome extension storage (chrome.storage). This storage is used only to provide the extension’s functionality. Your data stays on your device and/or within your Chrome profile (including Chrome Sync if you have it enabled).

Third-Party Sharing

Focus Blocker does not sell or share user data with third parties. The extension does not use analytics, tracking, advertising, or external servers.

Remote Code

Focus Blocker does not download or execute remote code. All logic is packaged within the extension.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer via the email listed on the Chrome Web Store listing.

Note: If features are added in the future that change data handling, this policy will be updated accordingly.
